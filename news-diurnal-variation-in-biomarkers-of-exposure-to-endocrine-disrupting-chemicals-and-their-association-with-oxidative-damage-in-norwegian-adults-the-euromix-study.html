Diurnal Variation in Biomarkers of Exposure to Endocrine-Disrupting Chemicals and Their Association with Oxidative Damage in Norwegian Adults: The EuroMix Study

- May 5, 2022

Are there unique chrono-windows of metabolism in humans for environmental chemicals like #phthalates, #parabens and #bisphenols? Well, there are some initial hints, but the human chronotoxicity of xenobiotics is a niche worth investigating further and in greater detail using the concept of #exposome. Check out our new collaborative work with the EuroMix Study and great colleagues from the Norwegian Institute of Public Health!

Read the full article here: https://www.mdpi.com

The Water & Health Laboratory is part of the Cyprus International Institute for Environmental and Public Health and its main goal is the study of health risk associated with chronic exposures to environmental chemicals with particular focus on the application of novel exposure assessment methodologies.
